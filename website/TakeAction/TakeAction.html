What Teachers Can Do

Encourage all students to try science and math, regardless of gender. Show more STEM female role models from different backgrounds in the classroom. Avoid using language such as "boys are better at it" to create a fair learning environment.

What Parents Can Do

Talk to your children about science and math more often, and don’t let gender limit their interests. Provide STEM toys and books to your children, regardless of gender. Give your children affirmation when they persist or solve problems, rather than emphasizing “talent.”
