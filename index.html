Breaking Stereotypes: Supporting Women and Girls in STEM

Introduction:

Welcome to my website! This site explores how stereotypes affect girls and young women's interest and confidence in STEM careers. They often receive messages from school, family, and the media that STEM is not for them. The site explores this issue from different perspectives in sections such as Background, Experience, Research Support, and Taking Action.

STEM
Science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) is an umbrella term used to group together the distinct but related technical disciplines of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
